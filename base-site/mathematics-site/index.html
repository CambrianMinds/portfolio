The question wasn't just theoretical. It was a matter of survival. How does connection mechanistically persist across spacetime? When loved ones are lost to death, or when eighteen months of forced separation breaks the physical reality of a relationship, what exactly remains?

This manuscript is the result of taking that deeply human question and demanding a rigorous, physical, cosmological answer. The silence couldn't negate the structure.
